Firebase Train Scheduler

Using Firebase for instantaneous updates, I have created an hypothetical Rocky Mountain Train Scheduler. The scheduler takes inputs from the user and replies with information on a train. The user enters the train name and its frequency and returns when the next train is expected and how long the user has to wait for its arrival.

Reagan's Bio

Reagan Duecker was born in Cedarburg WI. He attended Xavier University in Cincinnati, OH; graduating with a B.S in Business Management with a focus on Entrepreneurial Studies. After graduation, Reagan got interested in the fast past Oil and Gas Industry. Reagan started working with Calfrac Well Services as an operator in Williston, ND. After 2 years he was promoted to Field Engineer, the first without an Engineering Degree.

FIM Solutions has an unrelenting passion to improve oil and gas field operations, and truly believes mobile platform integration will allow substantial operational improvements. Using technics proven in tech development in Silicon Valley, FIM Solutions will leverage best practices and continuous development to ensure the end-user is being provided the best product available.
